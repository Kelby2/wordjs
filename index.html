Gameplay

Using the letters provided, enter as many words as you can find within the time limit (2 minutes).

All possible answers are on the side panels and will be revealed when you find them.

Answers are listed in alphabetical order.

Score additional points with longer words.

Words must be between 3-6 letters in length.

Controls

Enter to submit the word.
Spacebar to shuffle the letters.
Reveal All will end the game and display all answers.
Click New Word to start.
